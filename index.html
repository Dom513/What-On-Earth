How to play
  1. One player is the Alien. Everyone else are the Humans trying to guess.
  2. Hit "New round" to draw a fresh Earth card and Letter card — they stay hidden for now.
  3. When everyone's ready, press Start on the timer to reveal both cards and begin.
  4. The Alien describes the Earth concept in a sentence where word #1 starts with the Letter card's first letter, word #2 with the second letter, and so on. An underscore can be any letter the Alien chooses.
  5. No saying the actual words on the Earth card, and no skipping a letter!
  6. Humans shout out guesses until they get it. When the timer hits zero, both cards hide again automatically.
  7. Didn't like a draw? Hit "New Topic" or "New Letter card" to skip just that one, or "New round" to redraw both.
